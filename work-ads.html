Google Ads Optimisation

Portfolio

Campaign Objectives

  • Increase brand awareness and recognition within the target market.
  • Generate qualified leads and drive sales for products/services.
  • Decrease cost per click and sustain/increase conversion rates.

Challenges

  • Challenge 1: The client faced strong competition from established brands in their industry, requiring campaigns to be optimized for cost-effectiveness in online ad bidding.
  • Challenge 2: They had limitations with their existing website infrastructure, hindering their ability to create and manage dedicated landing pages for targeted campaigns. This necessitated the implementation of a more flexible hosting solution.

Solutions

  • Competitive Keyword & Bidding Strategy: To address the competitive landscape and manage bidding costs, we implemented a highly targeted keyword strategy focusing on long-tail keywords and specific product/service features. We also employed advanced bidding strategies, including target CPA and maximize conversions, constantly monitoring and adjusting bids to optimize performance and minimize costs.
  • Custom Hosting & Landing Page Development: To overcome the limitations of their existing website infrastructure, we utilised their current HubSpot account as a hosting solution and developed dedicated landing pages for each key campaign. These landing pages were designed with clear calls to action, compelling visuals, and optimized for conversions. This provided the necessary control and flexibility for targeted marketing efforts.
  • Ad Copywriting: Developed compelling ad copy that highlighted the client's unique selling points, such as their premium materials, quality and service using persuasive language and strong calls to action.
  • Campaign Structure: Implemented a structured campaign approach with targeted ad groups and keywords. Utilized various ad formats and employed remarketing campaigns.
  • Budget Allocation: Allocated budget strategically and regularly monitored and adjusted based on performance.
  • Tracking and Reporting: Implemented conversion tracking through HubSpot integration to provide instant feedback to the client.

Results

  • Cost-Effective Campaigns: By implementing targeted keyword and bidding strategies, we achieved a reduction from £8 to £0.6 in average (CPC) while maintaining consistent conversion rates. This significantly improved the (ROAS) and allowed for more efficient budget allocation.
  • Improved Lead Generation through Dedicated Landing Pages: The implementation of dedicated landing pages resulted in a 30% increase in lead generation compared to the previous period. The improved user experience and targeted messaging on the landing pages contributed to higher conversion rates.
  • Increased Website Traffic & Brand Visibility: Overall website traffic increased by 27% within three months, driven by the improved campaign performance and targeted landing pages. This led to increased brand visibility and engagement within the target market.
